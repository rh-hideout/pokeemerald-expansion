# full guide

## game intro
### inside player's home
1. Go straight ahead and upstairs to your room. 
2. Interact with the clock on your left hand side hanging on the wall.
   1. Set the minute hand by using the arrow keys. 
   2. Confirm the set time is synchronous with your real-life time.
   3. Talk to Bella.
3. Walk to your desk and interact with the book laying on top of it.
4. Walk downstairs again.

### inside PROFESSOR BIRCH's home
1. Walk outside the house by walking towards the orange matt in the bottom left hand corner.
2. Walk westwards to PROFESSOR BIRCH's home and enter through the front door.
3. Talk to PROFESSOR BIRCH's wife.
4. Walk upstairs.
5. Walk towards the Pokéball laying next to the bed and interact with it.
6. Talk to PROFESSOR BIRCH's child.

### fight against BUM
1. Leave the house again and head north.
2. A little girl is asking you for help.
3. Follow her request and head north a bit further.
4. Walk towards BUM and interact with him.
5. Talk to BUM.
6. Interact with the BAG to the BUM's left hand side.
7. Choose one of the three possible Pokéballs.
8. Talk to BUM.
9. The entity to the BUM's right hand side is a Zigzagoon. 
10. Fight the Zigzagoon by interacting with it.
    1. Press "Battle" and "Tackle" to damage your enemy.
    2. Your enemy will do the same.
    3. Repeat this process until your opponent faints.
11. Talk to BUM.

### travel to PROFESSOR BIRCH's child
1. Go home. 
2. Talk to BELLA to restore your Pokémon's health.
3. Leave your home and head north.
4. Walk past of the point of the fight with BUM and head east through the tall grass.
5. Directly pass the NPC and head northwest to find NEUOSTHEIM further north.
6. Visit the Pokémon Center.
   1. Talk to the woman behind the desk at the end of the room.
   2. Rest your Pokémon
   3. Exit the Pokémon Center
7. Head further north to enter Route 103.
8. Navigate through the tall grass, first east, then northwest.
9. Talk to PROFESSOR BIRCH's child.
10. Defeat PROFESSOR BIRCH's child in battle.
11. Talk to PROFESSOR BIRCH's child.

### travel to ctf
1. Head back south to JUNGBUSCH by skipping the cliffs.
2. Enter PROFESSOR BIRCH's laboratory.
3. Talk to PROFESSOR BIRCH.
4. Exit PROFESSOR BIRCH's laboratory.
5. Head back to Neuostheim.
6. Travel east towards the passage which is now accessible.

## CTF game
### flag 0
1. Reach the DHBW building.
2. Solve the puzzle to enter the building.
3. Talk to PROFESSOR FARMER.

### flag 1
1. Enter the museum.
2. Listen to the dialogue.
3. Talk to PROFESSOR FARMER to heal your Pokémon.
4. Talk to either JESSIE or JAMES.
5. Defeat JESSIE and JAMES.
6. Talk to the newly appeared NPC.
7. Solve the puzzle to exit the museum

### flag 3
`upon flag entry`
Enter the cave.
Read the hint carved in the wall.
Find a weird field on the ground.
Solve the puzzle.
Exit the first section of the cave.
Enter the second section of the cave.
Talk to the NPC on your right hand side.
Solve the puzzle to exit the cave.
Talk to KALI.
Defeat KALI.
