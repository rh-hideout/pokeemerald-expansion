# Characters

## Purpose

List only character facts already established by the handoff and canonical Spec.

## Current Canon

- **Zorua:** the young Pokémon protagonist who restores habitats, befriends Pokémon, and uncovers history.
- **Zoroark:** the lone caster of the Great Illusion, permanently faded into it ten years before play begins.
- **Groudon and Kyogre:** share a garden in the locked finale.
- **Rayquaza:** returns in the locked finale, wakes Deoxys by arriving, and chooses not to strike.
- **Deoxys:** awakens at the finale and is the opponent in the only mandatory battle.

## Notes

No relationship, biography, voice, motive, professor detail, elder detail, or reunion outcome is approved beyond these facts.
