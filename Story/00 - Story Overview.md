# Story Overview

## Purpose

Keep the approved premise and progression boundaries in one place. This is an organizational reference, not scene copy.

## Current Canon

Hoenn Habitat is a cozy Pokémon Emerald ROM hack in which befriending replaces capture. The player is a young Zorua. Ten years after Hoenn disappeared behind the Great Illusion, Zorua restores habitats, befriends the Pokémon that remained, and uncovers what happened.

The central question is: **Should Hoenn remain hidden?** The main-story ending and the all-habitats epilogue are distinct. The main story restores habitats and uncovers history. Only after every habitat is restored are the restored bonds sufficient for the veil to lift; the ferry returns, some trainers come home, and life continues.

## Notes
