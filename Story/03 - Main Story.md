## Purpose

## Current Canon

Zorua begins ten years after the Great Illusion, restores habitats, befriends the Pokémon that remained, and uncovers what happened to Hoenn. The central question is whether Hoenn should remain hidden. There is no villain.

The main-story finale is the locked Deoxys sequence in `02 - Locked Canon.md`. The all-habitats epilogue is a separate progression gate: restored bonds are sufficient for the veil to lift only after every habitat is restored.
