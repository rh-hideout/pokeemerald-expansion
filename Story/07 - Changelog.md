**2026-07-19 — v0.2 canonicalization.** Canonical authority moved to the design-and-story-readiness Spec v0.2; this Story set organizes that authority without adding prose.

**2026-07-19 — Ruling 1: timeline.** The sixteen-event pre-play order is locked, including Birth Island dormancy and the Sky Pillar sequence. **Supersedes:** the incomplete pre-play ordering that omitted those events.

**2026-07-19 — Ruling 2: Zoroark.** Zoroark permanently entered the Great Illusion before play begins. Its relationship to Zorua and history delivery remain open. **Supersedes:** the elder-present-at-opening premise.

**2026-07-19 — Ruling 3: conflict.** The main story has no villain. **Supersedes:** villain-led main-story framing.

**2026-07-19 — Ruling 4: finale.** The Deoxys encounter is the only mandatory battle, with the specified garden, weather, Rayquaza, restraint, and protection beats. **Supersedes:** non-finale battle-gating and an unspecified finale sequence.

**2026-07-19 — Ruling 5: epilogue.** Restored bonds after every habitat are the veil condition; the ferry returns and trainer returns are limited to some people. **Supersedes:** the universal trainer-homecoming assumption and an unqualified epilogue threshold.

**2026-07-19 — Ruling 6: evolution.** Evolution is not an epilogue prerequisite or cause; its timing remains open. **Supersedes:** the epilogue-causality proposal.

**2026-07-19 — Ruling 7: restraint.** Character biographies, dialogue, town, professor, tutorial, elder, and reunion details remain unresolved rather than being filled in for this documentation pass. **Supersedes:** connective fiction used to fill those gaps.
