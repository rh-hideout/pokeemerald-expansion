# Tone Guide

## Purpose

Preserve the approved register for later approved writing and visual review.

## Current Canon

Cozy comes first. The story may be melancholy, but is not hopeless. Use a legend register where appropriate and prefer concrete imagery over abstract mythology. There is no villain and no chosen-one framing.

## Notes

Do not bridge gaps with invented biographies, dialogue, town stories, professor details, elder details, reunion outcomes, or tutorial narrative. Record a gap as an Open Question instead.

## Future Expansion

**Proposal:** any new prose must receive narrative approval before it is added.
