# Locked Canon

## Purpose

Collect the rules that later narrative work must preserve.

## Current Canon

Authority source order is: (1) the approved Story Handoff; (2) the relevant
canonical Spec v0.2 sections when they do not conflict with that Handoff; (3)
the repository implementation state for what is currently built; and (4)
Proposals and Open Questions, which are non-canon. There is no villain. Zorua
restores habitats, befriends Pokémon, and uncovers Hoenn's history.

The finale beats are locked: Groudon and Kyogre share a garden; the weather clears; Rayquaza returns; Rayquaza's arrival awakens Deoxys; the only mandatory battle is Zorua versus Deoxys; Zorua protects rather than conquers; Deoxys lowers its attack; and Rayquaza chooses not to strike.

After every habitat has been restored, restored bonds are sufficient for the veil to lift.

Evolution is neither an epilogue prerequisite nor the cause of that result. The ferry returns and some trainers come home; individual reunion outcomes are not locked.

## Notes

Capturing Pokémon is not part of the game loop; befriending is the only acquisition path. Ordinary habitat encounters are not mandatory battles.

## Future Expansion

**Open Question:** whether and when Zorua evolves remains undecided.
